# Prompt background — Scène score capsule moderne

Create a premium modern football live score background inspired by clean social sports scorebugs and rounded capsule UI design, without copying any existing broadcast package.

Use case: stylized-concept.

Canvas/layout reference: 1920x1080.

Style:

- ultra-clean modern sports broadcast graphics;
- rounded capsule panels;
- minimal premium UI;
- high contrast;
- social media live scoreboard aesthetic;
- YouTube/OBS friendly;
- crisp glossy UI with soft shadows.

Visual identity colors:

- #E61D25 red;
- #3CAC3B green;
- #2A398D navy blue;
- #FFFFFF white;
- bright turquoise accent for score capsules.

Background:

- smooth neutral light gray to deep navy radial gradient;
- subtle broadcast texture;
- slight depth;
- no crowded stadium;
- no players;
- no flags;
- no fake logos.

Main layout:

Create one large horizontal score capsule centered around y=150 to y=315, inspired by a modern clean sports scorebug. The capsule system must feel premium, balanced, and readable.

Exact reserved empty zones:

- left team capsule: x=120, y=150, w=520, h=150, for Team 1 flag, name, and status dot.
- left score capsule: x=640, y=135, w=170, h=180, for Team 1 score.
- center logo zone: x=810, y=115, w=220, h=220, for the real Zone Mondial 26 logo inserted by HTML.
- right score capsule: x=1030, y=135, w=170, h=180, for Team 2 score.
- right team capsule: x=1200, y=150, w=520, h=150, for Team 2 name, status dot, and flag.
- minute/status capsule: x=1725, y=150, w=150, h=150, for match minute.
- bottom ticker safe zone: x=60, y=1005, w=1540, h=55.
- bottom right logo reserve: center x=1780, y=850, diameter 170.

Panel style:

- black rounded team capsules;
- bright turquoise score capsules;
- white rounded minute capsule;
- red/green/navy accent strips behind capsules;
- soft premium shadows;
- slight glossy reflection;
- clean crisp edges;
- high contrast for HTML text.

Logo rule:

- do not generate any logo;
- do not generate FIFA trophy;
- do not generate ZM26 text;
- leave the center logo zone empty for the real logo.png.

Important constraints:

- no readable text;
- no numbers;
- no team names;
- no flags;
- no logos;
- no trophy;
- no player faces;
- no fake UI labels;
- no watermark;
- leave all content areas empty for HTML overlay.

Negative prompt:

Readable text, numbers, flags, team names, player faces, fake logo, ZM26 logo, watermark, trophy, video thumbnails, crowded details, low contrast panels, blurry score zones.
