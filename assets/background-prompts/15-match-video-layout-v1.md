# Prompt background — Scène score + vidéo verticale 9:16

Create a premium 16:9 football live match scoreboard background with integrated vertical 9:16 video area for a YouTube sports broadcast, official visual identity of Zone Mondial 26.

Use case: stylized-concept.

Canvas/layout reference: 1920x1080.

Style:

- hybrid anime and postcard art;
- hand-drawn high-budget sports broadcast illustration;
- cinematic stadium atmosphere mixed with modern live sports studio;
- premium World Cup 2026 inspired broadcast package;
- energetic but clean and readable;
- professional TV sports overlay design.

Color palette:

- #E61D25 red;
- #3CAC3B green;
- #2A398D navy blue;
- #FFFFFF white.

Scene purpose:

Live match score overlay combined with a vertical social/video playlist.

Visual direction:

- dark navy blue stadium atmosphere;
- subtle floodlights from upper left and upper right;
- soft crowd silhouettes;
- elegant football field lines;
- premium glassmorphism broadcast panels;
- faint red and green light streaks;
- subtle blue data-grid accents;
- cinematic depth;
- polished live match center feeling;
- dynamic but not noisy.

Exact empty layout zones:

- Top center: x=500, y=70, w=920, h=88, thin horizontal empty glass panel for competition title and match status.
- Left main scoreboard panel: x=90, y=200, w=1050, h=640, large clean glass panel for the live match score.
- Inside left panel:
  - top team row: x=145, y=245, w=940, h=150, for Team 1 flag and name.
  - center score zone: x=270, y=410, w=690, h=190, for two large score numbers and central colon.
  - bottom team row: x=145, y=615, w=940, h=150, for Team 2 flag and name.
  - small lower info strip: x=210, y=775, w=810, h=55, for minute, TAB, qualified badge, or match info.
- Right vertical video frame: x=1245, y=210, w=405, h=720, exact 9:16 aspect ratio.
- Bottom ticker safe area: x=48, y=1018, w=1560, h=52.
- Bottom right logo zone: center x=1780, y=955, diameter 170.

Scoreboard panel requirements:

- left scoreboard panel must be clean, dark, and readable;
- score zone must have strong contrast for white HTML numbers;
- leave clear space between the two score numbers;
- central colon may be implied by subtle separator styling but do not generate actual text or numbers;
- team rows must support flags and long team names;
- lower info strip must support minute, TAB score, and qualified text.

Video frame requirements:

- right-side 9:16 frame must be empty and clean;
- dark neutral interior so HTML video can cover it fully;
- premium glass border around the vertical video;
- subtle red and green accent lights around the frame;
- do not draw video content, thumbnails, people, or fake UI inside the frame.

Panel design:

- subtle glassmorphism borders;
- dark transparent backgrounds;
- soft shadows;
- crisp broadcast edges;
- premium sports package textures;
- high contrast for white HTML text;
- consistent border design across all panels.

Logo rule:

- do not generate the logo;
- do not create any alternative logo;
- do not create ZM26 text or fake branding;
- keep a clean empty circular reserved zone at the bottom right for the real uploaded logo.png.

Important constraints:

- no readable text;
- no numbers;
- no team names;
- no flags;
- no player faces;
- no fake logos;
- no watermark;
- no fake UI labels;
- no video thumbnail content;
- no pre-rendered score;
- no pre-rendered table.

Negative prompt:

Readable text, numbers, flags, team names, player faces, fake logo, ZM26 logo, watermark, video thumbnails, UI labels, crowded details behind score, low contrast panels, blurry score zones.
