# Prompt background — Scène score flat capsule v2

Create a very simple flat vector-style football scoreboard background inspired by a clean horizontal capsule scorebug layout, with thick rounded shapes and bold borders, for HTML text overlay.

Use case: stylized-concept.

Canvas/layout reference: 1920x1080.

Style/medium:

- flat vector UI;
- simple geometric shapes;
- clean 2D graphic design;
- no 3D;
- no realism;
- no glossy reflections;
- no stadium;
- no texture-heavy broadcast package;
- use simple solid fills;
- thick outlines;
- soft but minimal flat shadows only.

Color palette:

- light gray background;
- black team capsules;
- bright turquoise score blocks;
- white minute capsule;
- accents using #E61D25 red, #3CAC3B green, #2A398D navy blue;
- optional soft purple accent.

Design inspiration:

A single long horizontal pill-shaped live scorebug: team capsule, score block, center logo placeholder, score block, team capsule, separate minute capsule. Keep it minimal, flat, bold, and readable.

Exact composition:

- Place the scoreboard strip horizontally across the upper-middle area, y=170 to y=330.
- Use very large rounded rectangles with approximately 40px to 65px border radius.
- Use thick flat outlines approximately 10px to 20px.
- Use a light neutral gray full background with very subtle radial gradient only.

Reserved empty zones:

- left team black capsule: x=90, y=185, w=560, h=130. Rounded black fill with thick white outline. Small empty rounded flag slot at far left, small circular status dot slot beside it, large empty text area.
- left score turquoise capsule: x=650, y=165, w=165, h=170. Bright turquoise fill, thick dark outline, rounded corners.
- center logo placeholder: x=815, y=145, w=210, h=210. White rounded vertical capsule or circle with thick black outline, completely empty inside for real logo inserted by HTML.
- right score turquoise capsule: x=1025, y=165, w=165, h=170. Bright turquoise fill, thick dark outline, rounded corners.
- right team black capsule: x=1190, y=185, w=560, h=130. Rounded black fill with thick white outline. Large empty text area, small circular status dot slot, small empty rounded flag slot at far right.
- minute white capsule: x=1760, y=185, w=140, h=130. White fill, thick light gray outline, rounded corners.
- bottom ticker flat capsule: x=90, y=995, w=1450, h=58. Dark navy flat rounded pill with red/green accent ends.
- bottom right logo reserve: center x=1780, y=880, diameter 170. Empty circle with thick white border.

Panel rules:

- all content zones must be empty;
- no text anywhere;
- no numbers anywhere;
- no flags;
- no logos;
- no trophy;
- no player faces;
- no fake UI labels;
- keep the center logo placeholder empty;
- keep the score capsules empty;
- keep the minute capsule empty.

Aesthetic constraints:

- flat simple design;
- bold rounded shapes;
- thick borders around 10px to 20px;
- minimal flat shadows only;
- no photorealistic lighting;
- no bevels;
- no metallic effects;
- no glassmorphism;
- no neon glow;
- no stadium background;
- no complex textures.

Negative prompt:

Readable text, numbers, flags, team names, player faces, logo, trophy, watermark, 3D render, glossy reflection, glass effect, stadium, heavy gradients, thin borders, clutter.
